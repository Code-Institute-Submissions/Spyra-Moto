The Roots

Wojtek comes from family that was involved in motorcycles since their started to cross our Globe. His Dad and his Uncle they had couple of them while Grandpa was a mechanic who fix their machines as well as worked for external customer and gained respect for his job.

Early infected

First bike in age 14. Many kilometers later started dreaming about having bike that gonna be really unusual and The Cyclop has raised.
Kept for number of years finaly found new home in UK. And after publication people started to phone if he could do same good job on their model.
After first, the second came, and so on. Today big family of over 20 beauties like "Skirtblower" or "N°13 Single Bend" are flying to new owners overseas.
